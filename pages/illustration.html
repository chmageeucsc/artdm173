Illustration

Name:

EnJOY the Journey

Brief:

This project tasks us with creating a promotional poster that accurately conveys a message to its audience. The main requirement is to utilize the various Adobe Illustrator techniques taught during the class.

Role:

Graphic Designer

Date:

Nov 2023 - Dec 2023

Approach:

For this project, I started by brainstorming quotes and messages to base my design around. I ended up choosing “Enjoy the Journey” as my quote.

It is meant to reassure the viewer that it is okay to enjoy life no matter how old they get. It takes various aspects of life that can be seen as indulgent or childish and overwhelms the viewer's senses with a colorfully whimsical scene.

Process:

Some sketches from my brainstorming sessions. I wanted a maximalist design that appreciates everything from food to art to hobbies.

A better visualization of what I wanted in my poster. Taking care of your inner child, even as you grow up, is very important to me.
