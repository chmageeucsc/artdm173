UXUI (User Experience and User Interface)

Name:

Kurzgesagt (app and website)

Brief:

The goal of this project is to create a conceptual app and supplementary website for an organization or business. This project allows us to become familiar with the design process from brainstorming to building.

Role:

UXUI Designer

Date:

Aug 2023 - Dec 2023

Approach:

For this project, I used Agile Methodologies to create an app based on the popular educational YouTube channel, Kurzgesagt. First, I identified the problem: We need more accessible resources for kids to learn.

Next, I researched potential competitors that solve similar problems and gather data on the type of person that would use my app. After establishing my audience, I began brainstorming possible features, layouts, and designs for the app.

Once I had a general wireframe, I began looking at color schemes, fonts, and iconography to use in my design. I designed, tested, adjusted, and retested until I created a prototype that my audience liked.

Process:

My biggest inspiration for Kurzgesagt was Duolingo's user interface. Each navigation tab had a purpose, and the main functions of the app were easy to figure out.

I used Figma to make my wireframes and prototype. After each iteration, I had a few people test out the flow and learnability of my app.
