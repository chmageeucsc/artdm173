The RVPA Creative Technology Intern program is an application-based opportunity that selects top Regional VirtualProduction Academy students pursuing their Fundamentals of Virtual Production Certificate of Achievement.


This internship program provides students with immersive, hands-on experience, industry connections, and practicalskills, equipping them for high-demand roles in virtual production, real-time technologies, and related fields.


The RVPA Creative Technology Internship bridges the gap between education and industry, ensuring students arewell-prepared to excel in the evolving landscape of digital content creation.
